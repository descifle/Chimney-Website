ABOUT

The Story of McDowell’s Complete Chimney Service, Inc.

McDowell’s was established in 1978, by Wayne McDowell, Jason Rieger (Wayne's nephew) Has been around the business since he was a kid. he eventually went on to work for Mcdowell's where he learned the Trained From wayne.

Now after 25 year's of working for Wayne, and for all of their customers in the community and surrounding areas, Jason bought the family business in November of 2019. This has been something he has worked hard for. and is happy to carry the torch. Mcdowells chimney Company hasn't changed otherwise. We are still your full service chimney Co. (Fireplace, Woodstove, Oil/gas Furnace & flue cleanings. Installing wood, and gas inserts/servicing. full Pre-Fab fireplace replacements.

Wayne is a certified member of the Chimney Safety Institute of America, National Chimney Sweep Guild, National Fireplace Institute, and is licensed by the Maryland Home Improvement Commission. We do chimney cap and chase cover top replacements as well as firebox panels, dampers, etc... We have a full time mason on staff for all of your Masonry repairs/Crowns (Rich Cole) We also install stainless Steel liner's for fireplace, Woodstoves Furnace/HW heaters etc...

Jason's wife, and mom work part time in the office. And Laura Stewart is the office manager with more than 15 years of experience for any of your technical questions. We are CSIA certified. Members of the National Chimney Sweep Guild, As well as a preferred company of the BBB. Mcdowells appreciate's all of their customers. And the opportunity to continue servicing your homes. Thank you
